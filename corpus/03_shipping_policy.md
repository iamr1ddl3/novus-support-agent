# Acmera Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods

Acmera offers three shipping options for domestic orders within India:

**Standard Shipping**: Delivered within 5-7 business days. Free on orders over ₹999. Orders under ₹999 incur a flat ₹99 shipping fee.

**Express Shipping**: Delivered within 2-3 business days. Available for ₹199 on all orders. Free for Premium Gold members.

**Same-Day Delivery**: Available in select metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune). Orders placed before 12:00 PM are delivered by 9:00 PM the same day. Same-day delivery costs ₹399 for all customers.

## Order Processing

Orders are processed during business hours (Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST). Orders placed after 6:00 PM are processed the next business day.

Premium members benefit from priority processing — their orders enter the fulfillment queue immediately upon payment confirmation, regardless of order volume.

## Shipping to Remote Areas

Delivery to remote or rural pin codes may take an additional 2-3 business days beyond the estimated delivery time. Remote area surcharges of ₹149 may apply and will be displayed at checkout before payment.

## Order Tracking

All shipments include tracking. Customers receive automated email and SMS updates at the following stages: order confirmed, order shipped, out for delivery, and delivered. Real-time tracking is available through the Acmera app or website.

## Damaged or Lost Shipments

If a package arrives damaged, customers should report it within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. Acmera will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

For packages marked as delivered but not received, customers should file a claim within 7 days. Our logistics team will investigate with the carrier. If the package cannot be located, a full refund or replacement will be issued within 10 business days.

## International Shipping

Acmera currently does not offer international shipping. Customers outside India can use third-party forwarding services at their own cost and risk. Acmera is not responsible for customs duties, taxes, or delays associated with international forwarding.
