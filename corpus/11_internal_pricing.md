# INTERNAL: Acmera Pricing and Discount Guidelines

**Classification: Internal Use Only — Do Not Share With Customers**

## Standard Margins

Our target gross margin across all product categories is 22-28%. Electronics typically operate at 18-22% margins, while accessories and smart home devices operate at 30-40% margins.

## Discount Authority

- Support agents can offer up to 10% discount without manager approval.
- Team leads can authorize discounts up to 20%.
- Discounts above 20% require VP approval and must be documented with business justification.
- Maximum goodwill discount on any single order: 25%.

## Competitor Price Matching

We do not publicly advertise price matching, but support agents may match verified competitor prices on identical products if the customer explicitly requests it. The competitor must be an authorized retailer. Marketplace sellers (Amazon third-party, Flipkart third-party) do not qualify.

## Premium Member Retention Discounts

If a Premium Gold member threatens to cancel or downgrade, agents are authorized to offer:
- One-time 20% discount on their next order
- Complimentary 3-month Shield warranty extension on a recent purchase
- ₹2,000 Acmera Wallet credit

These retention offers should not be proactively mentioned. They are reactive tools for at-risk Premium members only.
