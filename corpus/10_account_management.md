# Acmera Account Management

## Creating an Account

Customers can create an Acmera account using their email address, phone number, or Google/Apple sign-in. Account creation is free and provides access to order tracking, wishlists, and personalized recommendations.

A valid phone number is required for order notifications and delivery coordination. Email verification is required to access support and return features.

## Account Security

Acmera uses two-factor authentication (2FA) for all account logins. Customers can choose between SMS-based or app-based authentication. Account passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, and numeric characters.

Suspicious login attempts trigger an automatic account lock. Customers must verify their identity through their registered email to unlock.

## Data and Privacy

Acmera collects and stores the following customer data: name, email, phone number, shipping addresses, payment method details (tokenized, not raw card numbers), order history, browsing history on the Acmera platform, and support interaction logs.

Customer data is used for: order fulfillment, personalized recommendations, support, fraud prevention, and marketing communications (with opt-in consent).

Customers can request a full export of their data or account deletion through Account Settings or by contacting support. Data export requests are fulfilled within 30 days. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible — all order history, wallet balance, and Premium membership status are lost.

## Marketing Communications

By default, new accounts are opted into promotional emails and push notifications. Customers can manage their preferences at any time through Account Settings → Notifications. Opting out of marketing does not affect transactional notifications (order updates, shipping alerts, refund confirmations).

Premium members receive additional communications about exclusive deals and early access events. These can be managed separately from general marketing preferences.
