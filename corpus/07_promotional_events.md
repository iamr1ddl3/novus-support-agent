# Acmera Promotional Events Policy

## Types of Promotional Events

Acmera runs several types of promotional events throughout the year:

**Seasonal Sales**: Major sale events tied to festivals and holidays — Diwali Dhamaka (October/November), Republic Day Sale (January), Independence Day Sale (August), and Year-End Clearance (December). These typically run for 5-7 days.

**Flash Sales**: Short-duration sales lasting 6-24 hours, typically featuring a limited number of products at significant discounts (40-70% off). Flash sales are announced 24 hours in advance via app notifications and email.

**Clearance Events**: Quarterly clearance of end-of-line and overstocked products. Discounts range from 30-80%. Clearance items have limited stock and are sold on a first-come-first-served basis.

**Premium-Exclusive Sales**: Available only to Premium members. These events feature curated selections with exclusive pricing not available during general sales.

## Pricing During Promotions

All promotional pricing is clearly marked with the original price and discounted price displayed. Promotional discounts cannot be combined with coupon codes or other offers unless explicitly stated.

Price matching is not available during or after promotional events. If an item goes on sale within 7 days of your purchase, standard customers can request a one-time price adjustment. Premium members can request price adjustments within 14 days.

## Return Policy for Promotional Purchases

Items purchased during promotional events are subject to modified return terms:

- **Return window**: Reduced to 15 calendar days from delivery (standard is 30 days).
- **Restocking fee**: A 10% restocking fee applies to all returns of promotional items.
- **Final sale items**: Some clearance items may be marked as "Final Sale" and are not eligible for return or exchange. These are clearly labeled at the time of purchase.

**Premium Member Exception**: Premium members (both Silver and Gold) are exempt from the reduced return window and restocking fee. Their standard extended return windows apply to all promotional purchases. However, even Premium members cannot return items marked as "Final Sale."

## Inventory and Stock

Promotional items are available while supplies last. Acmera reserves the right to limit quantities per customer. Orders exceeding quantity limits may be partially or fully cancelled with a full refund.

Back-orders are not accepted during promotional events. If an item goes out of stock, it is removed from the sale.

## Cancellations

Orders placed during promotional events can be cancelled before shipment for a full refund. Once shipped, the promotional return policy applies. Cancellation requests must be made through the app or by contacting support.
