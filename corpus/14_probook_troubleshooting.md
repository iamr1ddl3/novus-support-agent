# Acmera ProBook Troubleshooting Guide

## ProBook Won't Turn On

1. Ensure the laptop is charged — connect the charger and wait 15 minutes before attempting to power on.
2. Perform a hard reset: hold the power button for 15 seconds, then release and press again.
3. Check if the charging LED is lit when the charger is connected. If not, try a different power outlet.
4. If the issue persists, it may be a hardware defect covered under warranty. Contact support.

## Battery Draining Quickly

1. Check battery health in Settings → System → Battery. If capacity is below 80%, it may be eligible for warranty replacement.
2. Close unused background applications.
3. Reduce display brightness and disable keyboard backlighting.
4. Check for firmware updates — battery optimization improvements are included in regular updates.
5. If battery health drops below 80% within the first year, this is considered a defect and is covered under standard warranty.

## Display Issues

1. For flickering: update your graphics driver through Settings → System → Display → Advanced.
2. For dead pixels: Acmera's dead pixel policy allows warranty claims if there are 5 or more dead pixels, or 1 or more in the center third of the display.
3. For color accuracy issues: calibrate your display through the built-in Acmera Display Calibration tool.

## Wi-Fi Connectivity Problems

1. Restart the laptop and your router.
2. Forget the network and reconnect.
3. Update the Wi-Fi driver through Device Manager.
4. If the laptop fails to detect any networks, it may indicate a hardware issue — contact support.

## Keyboard Not Responding

1. Restart the laptop.
2. Check if the issue is with specific keys or the entire keyboard.
3. For specific keys: clean gently with compressed air.
4. For the entire keyboard: try an external USB keyboard to verify it's a hardware issue.
5. If the internal keyboard has failed, this is covered under warranty for manufacturing defects.

## When to Contact Support vs. Return vs. Warranty

- **Within return window**: If the product has a defect and you're still within your return window (30 days standard, 45 days Premium Silver, 60 days Premium Gold), you can return for a full refund. This is often faster than a warranty claim.
- **After return window**: File a warranty claim through support. Repairs or replacements typically take 7-14 business days.
- **Physical damage**: Not covered by warranty. Contact support to discuss repair options (paid service).
