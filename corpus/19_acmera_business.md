# Acmera for Business

## Overview

Acmera for Business provides technology solutions for companies of all sizes. From equipping remote teams with laptops to outfitting offices with smart devices, we offer competitive pricing, dedicated support, and simplified procurement.

## Business Account Benefits

- GST-compliant invoicing on all orders
- Net-30 payment terms (subject to credit approval)
- Dedicated business account manager for accounts over ₹10 lakh annually
- Volume pricing (see Corporate Gifting program for bulk discounts)
- Asset tagging and inventory management tools
- Centralized billing across multiple offices

## IT Asset Management

For companies ordering 50+ devices, Acmera offers pre-configuration services:
- Operating system and software pre-installation
- Company WiFi and VPN pre-configuration
- Asset tag labeling and documentation
- Direct-to-desk shipping for distributed teams

Pre-configuration adds ₹500 per device and 3-5 business days to fulfillment.

## Business Support

Business accounts have priority support with a dedicated queue. Average response time: under 1 hour during business hours.

For critical issues affecting multiple devices or users, business accounts can request emergency support with a 15-minute response time (available 24/7 for accounts over ₹25 lakh annually).

## Return and Warranty for Business

Business orders follow the standard return policy with these additions:
- Bulk returns (10+ units) are handled by the dedicated account manager
- Advanced replacements are available for warranty claims — a replacement is shipped before the defective unit is returned (₹5,000 refundable deposit required)
- Extended warranty plans can be purchased for entire fleets at a 15% discount
