# Acmera AI Assistant & Smart Home Ecosystem

## Acmera AI Assistant

Acmera AI Assistant is the built-in voice and chat assistant available on the SmartHome Hub Pro and through the Acmera mobile app. It can help with:

- Controlling compatible smart home devices (lights, thermostats, cameras, locks)
- Playing music and podcasts through Acmera audio devices
- Setting reminders, timers, and calendar events
- Answering general knowledge questions
- Checking order status and tracking deliveries from your Acmera account

## Smart Home Compatibility

Acmera smart home products work with:
- **Acmera ecosystem**: Full native integration with all Acmera smart devices
- **Google Home**: Compatible via Google Home app pairing
- **Amazon Alexa**: Compatible via Alexa skill
- **Apple HomeKit**: SmartHome Camera 360 is HomeKit compatible. Hub Pro is not currently HomeKit compatible (planned for Q2 2026).

## Setup and Installation

**SmartHome Hub Pro**: Plug in and connect to WiFi. The Hub will automatically detect other Acmera smart devices on the same network. Non-Acmera devices can be added through the Devices section.

**SmartHome Camera 360**: Requires the Acmera app for initial setup. Scan the QR code on the camera base, connect to WiFi, and choose your storage option (local microSD or cloud).

## Cloud Storage Plans for Cameras

- **Free tier**: Live view only, no recording history
- **Basic (₹99/month)**: 7-day recording history, motion alerts
- **Premium (₹199/month)**: 30-day recording history, motion zones, person detection, package detection

Cloud storage plans are per camera. Multiple cameras require separate subscriptions unless bundled (3-camera bundle: ₹449/month for Premium on all three).

## Privacy and Data

Video footage is encrypted in transit and at rest. Acmera does not access or review customer video footage unless compelled by law enforcement with a valid legal order. Customers can delete all stored footage at any time through the app.

Voice interactions with Acmera AI Assistant are processed in the cloud. Customers can review and delete their voice history in Account Settings → Privacy → Voice Data.
