# Acmera Premium Membership Guide

## Overview

Acmera Premium is our loyalty program designed for frequent shoppers and long-term customers. Premium members enjoy a range of exclusive benefits across shopping, shipping, support, and account management. This guide outlines all benefits available to current Premium members.

## Membership Tiers

Acmera Premium has two tiers:

- **Premium Silver**: Automatically granted after spending ₹25,000 in a calendar year or maintaining an active account for 2+ years.
- **Premium Gold**: Granted after spending ₹75,000 in a calendar year or maintaining Premium Silver status for 3+ consecutive years.

Membership status is reviewed annually on the anniversary of enrollment. Members who fall below the spending threshold may be downgraded but will retain their current tier benefits for a 90-day grace period.

## Shipping Benefits

Premium Silver members receive free standard shipping on all orders over ₹500. Premium Gold members receive free express shipping on all orders regardless of value.

Both tiers receive priority processing, meaning orders are picked and shipped within 4 hours during business days. Standard customers typically experience 24-48 hour processing times.

During peak seasons (Diwali, end-of-year sales), Premium members' orders are prioritized over standard orders in the fulfillment queue.

## Early Access and Exclusive Deals

Premium members get 48-hour early access to all seasonal sales and new product launches. Premium Gold members additionally receive a quarterly exclusive discount code worth 15% off any single purchase.

Flash sales and clearance events are accessible to Premium members 24 hours before general availability. Some limited-edition products are exclusively available to Premium Gold members.

## Priority Support

Premium Silver members are routed to a dedicated support queue with an average response time of under 2 hours. Premium Gold members have access to a direct phone line staffed 24/7 with senior support agents.

All Premium members can escalate any support ticket directly to a team lead. Standard customers must go through the regular escalation process which typically takes 48-72 hours.

## Account Management

Premium members can store up to 10 shipping addresses and 5 payment methods. They also have access to detailed purchase analytics, including spending trends, category breakdowns, and personalized recommendations.

Premium Gold members are assigned a dedicated account manager who can assist with bulk orders, corporate gifting, and custom product requests.

## Return and Refund Privileges

This section outlines the enhanced return policies available exclusively to Premium members. These privileges are in addition to the standard return policy and override standard terms where conflicts exist.

### Extended Return Window

Premium Silver members receive a 45-day return window on all purchases, extended from the standard 30-day window. Premium Gold members receive a 60-day return window on all purchases.

The extended window is calculated from the delivery date, consistent with the standard policy. This extension applies to all product categories that are eligible for return under the standard policy.

Important: The extended return window applies retroactively to all purchases made while the customer holds active Premium status, even if the membership was activated after the purchase date.

### Electronics Return Exception

Standard policy requires electronics to be unopened for return eligibility. Premium members (both Silver and Gold) may return opened electronics within their extended return window, provided the item is in functional condition with all original accessories included.

This exception covers laptops, tablets, smartphones, headphones, and smart home devices. It does not cover consumable electronics accessories such as cables, screen protectors, or cases.

### Promotional Period Purchases

Items purchased during promotional events (flash sales, clearance, seasonal sales) are normally subject to a restricted 15-day return window with restocking fees. Premium members are exempt from both the reduced window and the restocking fee — their standard extended window applies to all promotional purchases.

### Refund Processing Priority

Refunds for Premium members are processed within 2 business days of the item being received at our warehouse, compared to 5-7 business days for standard customers. Premium Gold members may also request an instant store credit refund, which is issued immediately upon return shipment tracking confirmation.

### Return Shipping

Premium Silver members receive one free return shipping label per quarter. Premium Gold members receive unlimited free return shipping on all returns.

Standard customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a product defect or fulfillment error.
