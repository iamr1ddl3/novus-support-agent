# Acmera Corporate Gifting Program

## Overview

Acmera's Corporate Gifting program enables businesses to purchase products in bulk for employee rewards, client gifts, and event giveaways. The program is available to registered businesses with a valid GST number.

## Bulk Pricing

Orders of 10+ units qualify for bulk pricing:
- 10-49 units: 10% discount off retail price
- 50-99 units: 15% discount off retail price
- 100+ units: 20% discount off retail price, plus free express shipping

Custom quotes are available for orders exceeding 500 units. Contact corporate@acmera.com for enterprise pricing.

## Customization Options

Products can be customized with company logos or personalized messages for orders of 50+ units. Customization options include:
- Laser engraving on electronics (available for laptops, tablets)
- Custom packaging with company branding
- Personalized greeting cards included with each item

Customization adds 7-10 business days to fulfillment time. Customized items are not eligible for return or exchange.

## Corporate Accounts

Businesses can set up a corporate account with:
- Centralized billing with net-30 payment terms (subject to credit approval)
- Multiple shipping addresses for distributed teams
- Dedicated account manager for orders over ₹5 lakh annually
- Monthly spending reports and GST-compliant invoices

## Return Policy for Corporate Orders

Bulk orders follow the standard return policy with the following modifications:
- Returns must be for a minimum of 5 units (partial returns below this threshold are not accepted)
- The return window is 30 days from delivery regardless of any individual employee's Premium membership status
- Customized items cannot be returned
- Refunds for corporate orders are processed to the corporate account, not to individual recipients
