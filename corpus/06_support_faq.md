# Acmera Support FAQ

## Orders and Delivery

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: You can track your order through the Acmera app or website. Go to "My Orders" and select the order you want to track. You'll see real-time status updates and estimated delivery time. You also receive SMS and email notifications at each stage.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?**
A: Address changes are possible only if the order has not yet been shipped. Contact our support team immediately with your order number. Once the package is handed to the carrier, the delivery address cannot be modified.

**Q: Why is my order taking longer than expected?**
A: Delays can occur due to high demand periods, remote delivery locations, or carrier issues. Check your tracking information for the most current status. If your order is significantly delayed beyond the estimated window, contact support for assistance.

## Refund Procedure

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: The refund procedure begins once we receive and inspect your returned item. Processing takes 5-7 business days for standard customers and 2 business days for Premium members. The time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method — UPI is fastest (24 hours), cards take 5-7 days, net banking takes 7-10 days.

**Q: Can I get a refund to a different payment method?**
A: Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. We cannot redirect refunds to a different card, account, or payment method. The only exception is that Premium Gold members may opt for instant Acmera Wallet credit instead.

**Q: I returned an item but haven't received my refund yet.**
A: First, check that your return has been received at our warehouse — you can track this through the returns portal. If it shows as received and it's been more than 7 business days (or 3 business days for Premium members), contact support with your return tracking number.

## Account and Membership

**Q: How do I check my Premium membership status?**
A: Go to "Account Settings" → "Membership" in the app or website. You'll see your current tier, annual spending, and the date of your next status review.

**Q: Can I share my Premium benefits with family members?**
A: Premium benefits are tied to individual accounts and cannot be shared. However, Premium Gold members can add one secondary account holder who receives Premium Silver benefits. Contact your account manager to set this up.

**Q: What happens if my Premium status is downgraded?**
A: If you fall below the spending threshold at your annual review, you'll be notified 30 days before the downgrade takes effect. You'll have a 90-day grace period where you retain your current tier benefits. During this time, any additional spending counts toward reinstating your previous tier.

## Product Issues

**Q: My product arrived damaged. What should I do?**
A: Report the damage within 48 hours of delivery through the app or by contacting support. Include photos of the damaged product and packaging. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund — no need to return the damaged item in most cases.

**Q: Can I return a product bought during a sale?**
A: Sale items follow the standard return policy with one exception: items bought during promotional events (flash sales, clearance) have a reduced 15-day return window and may be subject to a 10% restocking fee. Premium members are exempt from both restrictions — their standard extended window and no restocking fee apply.

**Q: The product I want is out of stock. Can I be notified when it's back?**
A: Yes. On any product page, click "Notify Me" to receive an email and push notification when the item is restocked. Premium members receive notifications 24 hours before the general restock announcement.
