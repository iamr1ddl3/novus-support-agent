# Acmera Product Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All electronics sold by Acmera come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use conditions.

Warranty claims must be initiated through Acmera's support team, not directly with the manufacturer. This ensures consistent service and faster resolution.

## Extended Warranty Options

Customers can purchase extended warranty plans at the time of purchase or within 30 days of delivery:

- **Acmera Shield (2-year)**: Extends total coverage to 3 years. Costs 8% of product price.
- **Acmera Shield Plus (3-year)**: Extends total coverage to 4 years and includes accidental damage protection. Costs 12% of product price.

Extended warranty plans are non-refundable and non-transferable.

## What Is Covered

The standard warranty covers: hardware malfunctions, battery defects (if capacity drops below 80% within warranty period), display issues not caused by physical damage, and software defects present at the time of purchase.

## What Is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover: physical damage, water damage, damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs, normal wear and tear, cosmetic damage, or damage caused by use with non-compatible accessories.

## Warranty Claim Process

1. Contact Acmera support with your order number and description of the issue.
2. Our technical team will attempt remote diagnosis and troubleshooting.
3. If the issue cannot be resolved remotely, a prepaid shipping label will be provided for the defective item.
4. Repair or replacement typically takes 7-14 business days after receiving the item.
5. Customers will be notified if the item is to be repaired or replaced.

## Warranty vs. Return

The warranty and return policies are separate. A product can be returned within the return window regardless of warranty status. After the return window closes, warranty is the only recourse for defective products. Premium members' extended return windows do not extend the warranty period.
