# Acmera Return Policy

## Standard Return Window

All products purchased from Acmera are eligible for return within 30 calendar days of the delivery date. The return window begins on the date the item is marked as delivered by our shipping carrier, not the date of purchase.

To initiate a return, customers must contact our support team or use the self-service return portal at returns.acmera.com. A valid order number and the email address associated with the account are required.

## Conditions for Return

Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition. Products that show signs of wear, damage, or modification are not eligible for standard returns. Electronics must include all original accessories, cables, and documentation.

Opened software, digital downloads, and gift cards are not returnable under any circumstances. Perishable goods and personalized items are also excluded from our return policy.

## Refund Processing

Once a returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

For orders paid with credit card, please allow an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement after processing.

## Exchanges

Customers who prefer an exchange rather than a refund can request one during the return process. Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the replacement item is a different price, the difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.

## Holiday Season Extended Returns

Items purchased between November 15 and December 31 have an extended return window until January 31 of the following year. All other return conditions still apply during the extended window.
