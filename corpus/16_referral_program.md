# Acmera Referral Program

## How It Works

Existing Acmera customers can refer friends and earn rewards. Each customer receives a unique referral code available in their Account Settings.

When a new customer signs up using your referral code and makes their first purchase of ₹1,000 or more:
- **You (referrer)** receive ₹500 Acmera Wallet credit
- **They (referee)** receive ₹300 off their first order

## Referral Limits

Customers can refer up to 20 people per calendar year. There is no limit on the total rewards earned, but each individual referral must result in a genuine first purchase by a new customer.

Premium members earn enhanced referral rewards:
- Premium Silver: ₹750 per referral (instead of ₹500)
- Premium Gold: ₹1,000 per referral (instead of ₹500)

## Terms and Conditions

- The referred customer must be a genuinely new Acmera customer (no previous account).
- Self-referrals are not permitted. Acmera monitors for referral abuse and may terminate referral privileges.
- Referral wallet credits are promotional credits with a 90-day expiry.
- The referred customer's first order must not be cancelled or returned for the referral reward to be valid. If the order is returned, the referral credit is revoked.
- Referral rewards cannot be combined with other promotional offers on the referee's first order.
