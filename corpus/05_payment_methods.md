# Acmera Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods

Acmera accepts the following payment methods for online purchases:

**Credit and Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and RuPay. Both domestic and international cards are accepted. All card transactions are secured with 3D Secure authentication.

**UPI**: All major UPI apps are supported including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. UPI payments are processed instantly.

**Net Banking**: Available for all major Indian banks. Processing time is typically instant but may take up to 30 minutes during peak hours.

**Acmera Wallet**: Customers can load money into their Acmera Wallet and use it for faster checkout. Wallet balance can be loaded via UPI, net banking, or card. Minimum load amount is ₹100, maximum wallet balance is ₹50,000.

**EMI Options**: No-cost EMI is available on select products for orders above ₹5,000. EMI is available on credit cards from HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, and Kotak banks. Tenure options: 3, 6, 9, or 12 months depending on the product and card issuer.

**Cash on Delivery (COD)**: Available for orders between ₹200 and ₹25,000. A ₹49 COD convenience fee applies. COD is not available for same-day delivery orders or orders to remote pin codes.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails, the order is not placed and no amount is deducted. In rare cases where an amount is debited but the order is not confirmed, the refund is initiated automatically within 24 hours. Bank processing may take an additional 5-7 business days.

## Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. UPI refunds are processed within 24 hours. Card refunds take 5-7 business days. Net banking refunds take 7-10 business days. Wallet refunds are instant.

Premium Gold members can opt for instant store credit instead of waiting for payment method refunds.
