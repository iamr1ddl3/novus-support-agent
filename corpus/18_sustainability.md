# Acmera Sustainability Commitment

## Packaging

All Acmera products ship in 100% recyclable packaging. Since January 2025, we have eliminated single-use plastics from all shipments. Product packaging uses soy-based inks and FSC-certified cardboard.

For electronics, we use molded pulp inserts instead of expanded polystyrene (EPS). Our packaging is designed to protect products during transit while minimizing environmental impact.

## Product Lifecycle

Acmera offers a trade-in program for electronics. Customers can trade in old Acmera devices (or select competitor devices) for Acmera Wallet credit:

- Laptops: Up to ₹25,000 credit depending on condition and model
- Smartphones: Up to ₹10,000 credit
- Smart home devices: Up to ₹2,000 credit

Trade-in devices are refurbished and resold through the Acmera Renewed program, or responsibly recycled if beyond repair.

## Acmera Renewed

Refurbished products are sold at 30-50% discount with a 6-month warranty. Renewed products are graded:
- **Grade A**: Like new, minimal signs of use
- **Grade B**: Good condition, minor cosmetic wear
- **Grade C**: Functional, visible cosmetic wear

Renewed products follow the standard return policy. They are not eligible for extended warranty plans.

## Carbon Neutral Shipping

Acmera offsets 100% of shipping-related carbon emissions through verified carbon credit programs. Customers can see the estimated carbon footprint of their order at checkout.

Express and same-day deliveries have a higher carbon footprint. Customers who choose standard shipping receive a ₹10 Acmera Wallet credit as a thank-you for choosing the lower-emission option.
