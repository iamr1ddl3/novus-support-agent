# Acmera Wallet

## What is Acmera Wallet?

Acmera Wallet is a digital wallet built into your Acmera account. It allows for faster checkout, instant refunds (for eligible members), and can be used as a standalone payment method for any purchase on the platform.

## Loading Your Wallet

You can add funds to your Acmera Wallet using UPI, net banking, or credit/debit card. The minimum load amount is ₹100 and the maximum wallet balance is ₹50,000 at any time.

Wallet top-ups are processed instantly. There are no fees for loading money into the wallet.

## Using Your Wallet

Acmera Wallet can be used as the sole payment method or combined with another method. When combined, the wallet balance is applied first and the remaining amount is charged to the secondary method.

Wallet payments are processed instantly with no additional authentication required beyond your account login. This makes checkout significantly faster than card or UPI payments.

## Wallet Refunds

When a refund is issued to Acmera Wallet, the credit appears instantly. This is the fastest refund method available. Premium Gold members can choose to receive all refunds as wallet credit regardless of the original payment method.

## Wallet Expiry and Withdrawal

Wallet balance does not expire as long as the account remains active. If an account is inactive for 12 consecutive months, a notification is sent. After 18 months of inactivity, the wallet balance is forfeited.

Wallet balance cannot be withdrawn as cash or transferred to a bank account. It can only be used for purchases on the Acmera platform. If a customer deletes their account, any remaining wallet balance is forfeited.

## Promotional Wallet Credits

Acmera occasionally issues promotional wallet credits through marketing campaigns, referral programs, or as compensation. Promotional credits have a 90-day expiry from the date of issue and are applied before regular wallet balance during checkout.
